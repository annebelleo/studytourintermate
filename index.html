Explore new places

Every two summers, the study association Intermate travels to a far-away place to explore the area and learn about the companies there. The trip is 2 to 3 weeks long depending on the location. This year, we will be traveling to Scandinavia. Click the button below to learn more about our Study Tour 2021!

Visit local companies and universities

On each trip, we visit companies that are relevant for our department and compose a significant part of the country's economy. We also visit local universities. In the past, we have visited Amanah Ikhtiar Malaysia, Roundtable on Sustainable Palm Oil (RSPO), and Asia Pacific University.

Make new friends

Joining the trip will be students from the master programs Human Technology Interaction and Innovation Sciences as well as the bachelor programs Sustainable Innovation and Psychology & Technology. They are enthusiatic to travel to new places with you!
