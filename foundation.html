Foundation

Oversees the committee.

What is the foundation?

A Study Tour committee does not stand fully responsible for everything that happens during the organization of the Study Tour! An external organ was set up to check and control all important decisions made by the Study Tour under the name the Study Tour Foundation, or Foundation. This Foundation stands separate from SV Intermate.

The Foundation is enrolled in the chamber of commerce and exists out of a minimum of two board members of SV Intermate and the chair of the Study Tour committee! The remaining members of the Foundation were part of previous Study Tour committees. With the knowledge they acquired during their committee time, they can advice the current Study Tour committee on tough choices and decisions.

The committee regularly meets with the Foundation in order to discuss a variety of topics such as planning, go/nogo moments and the budget. Also the realization needs to be approved and checked by the Foundation. This is explicitly not done by the FCC of Intermate, as the Study Tour is a separate entity.

This year the Foundation will be more intensively used due to the corona crisis situation. Their advice helps the committee to decide on how safe it is within a country, and if it is responsible to go on the trip. This ensures a better thought out scenario plan, which covers most risks.
